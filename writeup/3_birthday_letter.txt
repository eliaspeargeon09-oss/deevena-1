THE HEARTFELT 19TH BIRTHDAY LETTER FROM ELIA
========================================================================

RECIPIENT: Dearest Deevena (My Chinakka)

Happy 19th Birthday! It feels absolutely insane that you are already nineteen! Even though you will always brag about being bigger than me (and yes, technically you are older and taller!), you will always remain my cherished sister.

Looking back, you have been so much more than just an older sibling. You have been a guiding anchor, a faithful pray-er, and a steady hand guiding my spirit. I admire how your heart seeks Jesus in everything, and how you naturally bring warmth and joy anywhere you set your foot. You make our home a brighter place of grace.

On this special checkpoint of 19th year of your life, I declare that God’s hand is holding your future. Keep singing, keep keeping our surrounding cleanly beautiful (as you always love to clean!), and keep shining with unshakeable faith.

With all my love and blessings,

Your loving brother, Elia
