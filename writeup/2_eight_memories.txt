========================================================================
STORY BOOK: THE EIGHT MEMORIES OF GRACE
========================================================================

MEMORIES 1 OF 8: The Anchor of Prayer
"Our shared quiet times and soft whispers of prayer. You've always lifted me closer to God's heart, Chinakka. Deevena, you are my ultimate spiritual guide."

MEMORIES 2 OF 8: Midnight Tea & Sweet Whispers
"Those late-night spiritual chats and listening to beautiful church chants together. Sharing a warm mug of ginger tea and laughing at inside jokes until our stomachs ache."

MEMORIES 3 OF 8: Walk of Faith
"Whenever we stroll through green trees, admiring His creation. You have always been the one to whisper: 'Keep your eyes on the cross, Elia.'"

MEMORIES 4 OF 8: Overflowing Joy
"The silly laughs where we couldn't even breathe! You have this glowing light inside that lights up every gloomy room. Turning 19 doesn't change your precious child-like heart."

MEMORIES 5 OF 8: Safe Harbor in Storms
"When things were heavy and I felt lost, your sisterly counsel and comforting prayers were my fortress. Thank you for never turning away when I needed you most."

MEMORIES 6 OF 8: Wholesome Teasing
"Yes, you are 19 now, and yes, you are older (and still a bit taller!). But I am always here to play the annoying younger brother and keep your life exciting!"

MEMORIES 7 OF 8: Melodies of Divine Grace
"Glorifying God side-by-side, sharing testimonies and singing holy hymns. Spreading the Gospel together is the highest privilege of being your brother in spirit."

MEMORIES 8 OF 8: A Blessed Destiny
"At nineteen, your steps are fully destined for His glory. May you continue to shine, grow, clean everything you see, and remain my absolute favorite sister. Happy Birthday, Chinakka!"
